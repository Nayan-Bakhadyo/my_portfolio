Intro

Satellite Research Fellow at Antarikchya Pratisthan Nepal. Researched and Developed edge-AI model on image classification and segmentation for MUNAL - High School Consortium Satellite Project which awaits launch from Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO). Project lead for 'Satellite Data Analytics Project (SDAP)' and 'Space-enabled Urban Solutions in Nepal (SUSN),' the latter in collaboration with Kathmandu Metropolitan City (KMC) and recognized at the Paris Peace Forum. Excited about the intersection of technology and societal impact, eager to contribute my skills and leadership to innovative projects that makes a difference..

Work

As a Satellite Research Fellow, led projects such as Space-enabled Urban Solutions in Nepal (SUSN) and the Satellite Data Analytics Project (SDAP) involving collaboration with key organizations like Nepal Academy of Science and Technology (NAST) and Kathmandu Metropolitan City (KMC). My experience spans from developing edge-AI models for Munal Satellite, AI implementation for traffic analysis to research on population density using satellite data.

About
